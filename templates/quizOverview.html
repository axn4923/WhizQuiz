Quiz Overview

The quiz purpose is to test computer architecture knowledge, and concentrates on Data paths and control, as well as the components of a CPU. The quizzes represented are multiple-choice and prompt whether the answer is correct/incorrect after each question answered. The quizzes also provide feedback as to why the answer chosen is correct/incorrect. The overall goal is to offer students a variety of quizzes to test and educate students.
