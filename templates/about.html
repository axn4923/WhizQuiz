Introduction

The purpose of this website is to help students gain a deeper understanding of topics in computer architecture. This website concentrates on processor datapaths and control and pipelining. We want to create a learning application in order to strengthen the knowledge of students By providing educational information and offering quizzes that will help sharpen the learned material. Part of learning a new concept is relating to the material and having fun with it. We want to use this idea to make our learning platform as productive and successful for students as possible. We expect users to enjoy our style of quizzes and the way in which we explain certain topics, thus helping students gain a firmer grasp on topics within computer architecture. The quizzes are multiple-choice and prompt whether the answer is correct/incorrect after each question answered. The quizzes also provide feedback as to why the answer is correct/incorrect.
